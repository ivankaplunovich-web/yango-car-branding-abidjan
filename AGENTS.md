# AGENTS.md

## Project Overview

This project is a web-based landing dashboard for Yango focused on selling commercial car branding advertising in Abidjan.

The product should present a clear landing page experience combined with an embedded calculator. The calculator helps advertiser-side users estimate campaign value, branded fleet visibility, and expected media impact from placing Yango commercial advertisements on cars in circulation.

## Product Purpose

The dashboard is intended to:

- explain the car branding advertising offer in a simple and trustworthy way;
- show the business value of using Yango cars as a moving advertising surface in Abidjan;
- help brands estimate campaign scale, visibility, and package value;
- encourage companies to request a proposal, book a campaign, or contact sales.

## Core Concept

The website should function as both:

1. a marketing landing page;
2. an interactive calculator dashboard.

The landing section should communicate the offer, benefits, requirements, and process.  
The calculator section should let users enter key assumptions and instantly see estimated results.

## Target Audience

The primary audience is:

- companies and brands in Abidjan that want to buy car branding advertisement;
- marketing teams currently investing in social media advertising;
- businesses using out-of-home channels such as billboards;
- advertisers looking for a more mobile and city-visible branding format.

## Expected Calculator Inputs

Possible calculator fields may include:

- fixed market: Abidjan;
- campaign package or placement format;
- number of branded cars;
- branding format or advertisement package;
- campaign duration;
- estimated daily or monthly reach assumptions;
- estimated price, CPM, or media value.

## Expected Outputs

The calculator should clearly present:

- estimated campaign value;
- estimated visibility or impression range;
- estimated cost efficiency compared with other media;
- campaign summary for advertiser decision-making.

## Tone And UX Direction

The product should feel:

- modern;
- trustworthy;
- simple;
- premium but accessible for business buyers;
- optimized for both desktop and mobile web.

The interface should emphasize clarity, fast comprehension, and strong call-to-action placement.

## Business Context

This is a Yango-focused promotional and conversion tool for a car branding advertising product in the ride-hailing taxi category. The main use case is helping companies in Abidjan understand the value of using Yango cars as branded advertising assets instead of relying only on social media or traditional OOH formats such as billboards.

## Brand Guidance

The workspace includes a Yango brand playbook. The landing page and calculator should follow these core brand cues:

- primary brand red: `#FF0000`;
- black: `#000000`;
- white: `#FFFFFF`;
- supporting neutrals: `#EAECEC`, `#BEBFBE`, `#EBECEB`, and `#646464`.

Typography cues from the playbook:

- use `Yango Text Bold` or the closest available equivalent for prominent headings;
- use `Yango Text` or the closest available equivalent for body copy and UI text;
- keep typography bold, clean, and highly legible.

The overall visual language should stay minimal, high-contrast, and modern. Red should be used intentionally for emphasis, key calls to action, and branded highlights rather than flooding the whole page.

## Visual Reference Notes

The car wrap images in the workspace are reference material only. They are not Yango vehicles and should not be copied literally. They are useful for understanding what works well for moving ad formats:

- wraps need large high-contrast message zones that remain readable from a distance;
- layouts should prioritize side-door readability because that is the main viewing angle in traffic;
- the strongest examples use simple shapes, short text, and one dominant focal area instead of many small details;
- branding should feel visible in motion, not designed like a static poster;
- the car body should still look clean and premium after applying the wrap.

For this project, use the references to inform layout principles only. Do not reuse other brands' copy, colors, logos, gradients, or compositions.

## UX Implications For This Landing

Because the audience is advertiser-side and the product is visual media inventory, the landing should:

- quickly show what a branded Yango car campaign looks like in the city;
- compare car branding with social media and billboard advertising in a business-friendly way;
- make the calculator feel like a media planning tool, not a driver earnings tool;
- support visual proof, mockups, and campaign examples near the calculator;
- keep lead capture and sales contact actions visible throughout the page.

## Implementation Notes

- Build as a web-based dashboard / landing page.
- Keep the calculator prominent and easy to use.
- Make conversion actions visible, such as "Request Proposal", "Book Campaign", or "Get Started".
- Prioritize responsive design and quick loading.
- Structure content so the project can later expand with more pricing logic, localization, lead capture forms, and campaign mockup galleries.
